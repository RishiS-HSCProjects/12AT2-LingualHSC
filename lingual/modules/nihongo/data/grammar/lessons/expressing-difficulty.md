---

title: Expressing Ease and Difficulty in Japanese using やすい and にくい
summary: Learn how to express ease and difficulty in Japanese using the verb suffixes やすい and にくい.

---

In Japanese, you can express how easy or difficult an action is by adding やすい (easy) or にくい (difficult) to the stem of a verb.

:::blockquote Verb (stem) + やすい / にくい :::

- この本[ほん]は読[よ]み**やすい**です。→ This book is easy to read.
- この料[りょう]理[り]は作[つく]り**やすい**です。→ This dish is easy to make.
- この問題[もんだい]は解決[かいけつ]し**にくい**です。→ This problem is difficult to solve.
- この道[みち]は歩[ある]き**にくい**です。→ This road is difficult to walk on.
