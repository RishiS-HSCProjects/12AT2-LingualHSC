The potential form in Japanese is used to express the **ability or possibility** to do something.

/w Note: Potential form is explicitly used to indicate capability or possibility, not [permission](grammar:asking-giving-permission). \

# Potential Form of Godan Verbs
To form the potential form of Godan verbs, change the final う・い sound to its corresponding -え sound and add る.

| Dictionary Form | Potential Form |　Meaning        |
|-----------------|----------------|-----------------|
| 書[か]く        | 書ける          | Can write      |
| 飲[の]む        | 飲める          | Can drink      |
| 話[はな]す      | 話せる          | Can speak      |
| 待[も]つ        | 待てる          | Can wait       |
| 死[し]ぬ        | 死ねる          | Can die        |
| 遊[あそ]ぶ       | 遊べる         | Can play       |
| 買[かう]う       | 買える         | Can buy        |
| 泳[およ]ぐ       | 泳げる         | Can swim       |
| 取[と]る         | 取れる         | Can take       |
| 行く             | 行ける         | Can go         |

# Potential Form of Ichidan Verbs
To form the potential form of Ichidan verbs, simply replace the final る with られる.

| Dictionary Form | Potential Form | Meaning        |
|-----------------|----------------|----------------|
| 食[た]べる      | 食べられる     | Can eat        |
| 見[み]る        | 見られる       | Can see        |
| 起[お]きる      | 起きられる     | Can wake up    |
| 寝[ね]る        | 寝られる       | Can sleep      |
| 教[おし]える    | 教えられる     | Can teach      |
| 借[か]りる      | 借りられる     | Can borrow     |
| 着[き]る        | 着られる       | Can wear       |
| 出[で]る        | 出られる       | Can exit       |
| 覚[おぼ]える    | 覚えられる     | Can remember   |
| 降[ふ]る        | 降られる       | Can fall (rain)|

# Potential Form of Irregular Verbs

| Dictionary Form  | Potential Form | Meaning        |
|------------------|----------------|----------------|
| する             | できる      | Can do         |
| 来[く]る         | 来[こ]られる    | Can come       |
| 行[い]く         | 行[い]ける      | Can go         |

/t 行く works as an Godan verb in the potential form. \

# Using ことができる
Another way to express ability or possibility is by using the phrase ことができる after the verb in its dictionary form Many times, this construction can be simpler to say and use.

| Dictionary Form | ことができる Form | Meaning        |
|-----------------|-------------------|----------------|
| 書[か]く        | 書くことができる   | Can write      |
| 食[た]べる      | 食べることができる | Can eat        |
| する           | することができる   | Can do         |
| 来[く]る       | 来ることができる   | Can come       |
| 行[い]く       | 行くことができる   | Can go         |
