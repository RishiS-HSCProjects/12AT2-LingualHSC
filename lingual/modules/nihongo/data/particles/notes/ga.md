The particle **が** marks the **subject** doing the action or the thing being identified.

## Core Idea
- Often introduces new or specific information.
- Common in answers to "who/what" questions.

## Example

- だれ**が**来ましたか → Who came?
- 田中さん**が**来ました → Tanaka came.

## HSC Tip
If you need to highlight *who/what specifically*, **が** is usually a strong choice.
