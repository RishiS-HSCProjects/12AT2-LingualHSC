The particle **は** marks the **topic** of the sentence.

## Core Idea

- Think of it like: "As for ..."
- It sets context, then says something about that context.

## Example

- 私**は**学生です → As for me, I am a student.
- 猫**は**かわいい → As for cats, they are cute.

## HSC Tip
Use **は** when introducing or contrasting topics.
